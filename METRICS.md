# Product Analytics & North Star Definition

## The North Star Metric
Our North Star Metric is **Aggregate Weekly Defensible Savings Discovered (USD)**. 
We prioritize this over simple Daily Active Users (DAU) because this tool is a utility designed for periodic evaluation. Measuring the absolute financial volume of overspend discovered proves the tool is successfully surfacing the exact pain point that drives users to book a Credex consultation.

## Core Driver Metrics (Input Metrics)
1. **Form Completion Rate:** The percentage of users landing on the page who complete the entire form workflow rather than dropping off at the tool selection stage.
2. **Viral Share Multiplier:** The number of unique shared audit URLs generated per 100 completed reports.
3. **High-Savings CTA Conversion:** The click-through rate on the "Book Savings Consultation" button among profiles showing >$500/mo in waste.

## Hard Pivot Threshold
If our **Form Completion Rate falls below 40%**, it signals that our behavioral questions (activity level and repository scale) are causing too much onboarding friction. If that happens, we will pivot to an ultra-stripped form layout that relies entirely on an instantly scannable, single-row quick calculator layout.
